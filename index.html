MARKETING

The action or business of promoting and selling products or services, including market research and advertising.Marketing refers to activities a company undertakes to promote the buying or selling of a product, service, or good. It is one of the primary components of business management and commerce. Marketers can direct their product to other businesses or directly to consumers.
